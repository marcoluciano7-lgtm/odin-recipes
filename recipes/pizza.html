Pizza

Description

Pizza is a popular Italian dish made with dough, tomato sauce, cheese, and various toppings.

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough
  • Tomato sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Pepperoni

Steps

  1. Prepare the dough
  2. Add tomato sauce and toppings
